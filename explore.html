Explore Pune

✦

Discover the best of Pune — all within easy reach of Hotel Sahara

Hotel Sahara's prime location on Senapati Bapat Road puts you at the centre of Pune. From centuries-old forts to buzzing café districts, here's what to explore during your stay.

  Practical Tips

  • Our front desk arranges taxis and auto-rickshaws 24/7
  • Pune Metro (Shivajinagar station) is ~2km away
  • Best time to visit forts: early morning (6–9 AM)
  • Carry cash for local markets — many vendors are card-free
  • Monsoon (June–September) makes hills and forts lush and beautiful
  • Call us for a trusted cab driver: 020-25655405
Heritage

Shaniwar Wada

The iconic 18th-century Peshwa fortress is one of Pune's most visited landmarks. Explore its grand gates, ancient cannons, and beautifully lit sound-and-light show in the evenings.

~4 km 8 AM – 6:30 PM ₹25 entry
Heritage

Aga Khan Palace

A symbol of India's freedom movement — this stunning palace was where Mahatma Gandhi and Kasturba Gandhi were interned. Its sprawling lawns and Italian arches are breathtaking.

~8 km 9 AM – 5:30 PM ₹25 entry
Heritage

Raja Kelkar Museum

Home to one of India's most fascinating private collections — over 20,000 artefacts including antique lamps, miniature paintings, musical instruments, and rare medieval objects.

~5 km 10 AM – 5:30 PM ₹100 entry
Heritage

Parvati Hill & Temple

Climb 103 steps to reach this hilltop temple complex with panoramic views over Pune. One of the oldest heritage structures in the city, it's a perfect sunrise spot.

~5 km 6 AM – 8 PM Free entry
Nature

Sinhagad Fort

One of Maharashtra's most iconic hill forts — a popular trek with sweeping Sahyadri mountain views. Don't miss the famous local 'pithla-bhakri' food stalls at the top.

~35 km Sunrise – Sunset Moderate trek
Nature

Khadakwasla Dam

A scenic reservoir on the outskirts of Pune — ideal for an evening drive, sunset picnics, and photography. Especially spectacular during and after the monsoon season.

~20 km All day Free entry
Nature

Mulshi Lake & Backwaters

A serene escape into the Western Ghats — lush green hills, a vast lake, and quiet villages. Perfect for a day trip, boating, or simply unwinding in nature.

~45 km All day Day trip
Shopping

FC Road & JM Road

Pune's most vibrant streets — lined with boutique stores, bookshops, street food stalls, and cafés. A favourite haunt of students and young professionals. Perfect for an evening stroll.

~2 km 10 AM – 11 PM Walking distance
Shopping

Westend Mall

A popular multi-level mall right in the heart of the city with fashion brands, a food court, cinema, and entertainment — a one-stop destination for leisure shopping.

~3 km 10 AM – 10 PM
Shopping

Laxmi Road & Tulsi Baug

Pune's oldest and busiest market area — famous for traditional Maharashtrian jewellery, textiles, sarees, kitchenware, and religious items at great prices.

~5 km 10 AM – 9 PM Best deals in Pune
Dining

Koregaon Park Café District

Pune's trendiest neighbourhood — home to upscale restaurants, rooftop cafés, art galleries, and boutique stores. The famous German Bakery and many acclaimed eateries are here.

~7 km Noon – Midnight
Dining

Camp Area & MG Road

Pune's cosmopolitan zone — full of Irani cafés, iconic bakeries (try Kayani or Dorabjee's), multicuisine restaurants, and bars. East Street is a must-walk for food lovers.

~6 km 8 AM – 11 PM
Sports

Balewadi Sports Complex

A world-class multi-sport facility — venue of the 2008 Commonwealth Youth Games. It hosts cricket, football, swimming, and athletics events throughout the year.

~4 km 6 AM – 10 PM
Convention

International Convention Centre

Directly opposite Hotel Sahara — Pune's premier venue for conferences, trade shows, exhibitions, and cultural events. Perfect for business travellers attending ICC events.

Opposite hotel 2-minute walk
Family

Rajiv Gandhi Zoological Park

Home to leopards, bears, crocodiles, snakes and hundreds of bird species — this well-maintained zoo and snake park is great for families and nature enthusiasts.

~15 km 9:30 AM – 5:30 PM ₹30 entry
Wellness

Osho International Meditation Resort

One of the world's most visited meditation centres — a lush green oasis in Koregaon Park offering daily meditation sessions, spa treatments, and mindfulness programmes.

~7 km 9 AM – 9 PM

About Hotel Sahara

✦

A tradition of warmth, comfort, and gracious hospitality in Pune

A Jewel in the Heart of Pune

✦

Hotel Sahara has been welcoming guests to Pune for decades, building a reputation for genuine hospitality, well-appointed rooms, and attentive service. Situated on the prestigious Senapati Bapat Road, directly opposite the International Convention Centre, we are at the crossroads of Pune's business and cultural life.

Our promise is simple: to make every guest feel completely at home. Whether you are visiting Pune for a business conference, an academic event, or leisure travel, we ensure your stay is comfortable, enjoyable, and truly memorable.

At Hotel Sahara, you will find spacious, well-furnished rooms equipped with modern amenities, a restaurant offering delicious vegetarian cuisine, and a team ready to assist you around the clock.

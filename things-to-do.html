Explore Pune

✦

Discover the best of Pune — from our doorstep on Senapati Bapat Road

Hotel Sahara sits at the heart of Pune on prestigious Senapati Bapat Road, putting you minutes from temples, IT parks, universities, forts, and the city's finest dining and shopping. Here's our guide to making the most of your stay.

📍
Right Outside Your Door

Chaturshringi Temple

0.2 km

One of Pune's most revered hilltop temples dedicated to Goddess Chaturshringi. A quick walk from the hotel — best visited at sunrise or during Navratri.

Must VisitTemple5 min walk

International Convention Centre (ICC)

Opposite

One of India's premier convention and event spaces. Ideal if you're attending a conference, trade show, or corporate event — literally across the road.

BusinessEvents

Senapati Bapat Road

On doorstep

Pune's vibrant commercial artery lined with cafés, restaurants, banks, ATMs, and shops. Perfect for an evening stroll or quick errands.

FoodShoppingCafés

Pune University Campus

1.5 km

A sprawling, lush green campus spread over 400 acres. A peaceful place to walk or jog, with beautiful Raj-era architecture and gardens.

NatureWalk

FC Road (Fergusson College Road)

2 km

Pune's iconic student hub — packed with bookshops, street food stalls, casual dining, and indie boutiques. Great for people-watching and snacks.

FoodShoppingCasual

Balewadi Sports Complex

3 km

Pune's major sporting venue — home to cricket, football, and athletics events. Check local listings for matches during your stay.

SportsEvents
🏛️
Culture & Heritage

Shaniwar Wada

6 km

The iconic 18th-century fort palace of the Peshwa rulers. A UNESCO-recognised heritage site with a spectacular sound and light show in the evenings.

Top AttractionHistoryEvening Show

Dagdusheth Halwai Ganpati

6 km

One of Maharashtra's most famous Ganesh temples. The idol is adorned with gold and precious gems — a deeply spiritual and visually stunning experience.

Must VisitTemple

Aga Khan Palace

8 km

A magnificent palace with historical significance — Mahatma Gandhi and Kasturba Gandhi were detained here during India's freedom struggle. Serene gardens surround it.

HistoryGardens

Raja Dinkar Kelkar Museum

6.5 km

A treasure trove of over 20,000 artefacts collected by Dr. D.B. Kelkar — from medieval lamps and locks to miniature paintings and musical instruments.

MuseumArt

Osho International Meditation Resort

7 km

A world-famous wellness retreat offering meditation programmes in lush surroundings. Visitors can attend morning meditations and explore the beautiful campus.

WellnessMeditation

Parvati Hill Temple

4 km

A hilltop complex with panoramic views of Pune, ancient temples, and a museum with Peshwa-era artefacts. Best visited in the cooler morning hours.

TempleViewsHistory

Nature & Outdoors

Pune University Botanical Garden

1.5 km

A peaceful green oasis ideal for morning walks, jogging, and birdwatching within the university campus.

WalkNature

Katraj Snake Park & Zoo

12 km

A well-maintained zoo and reptile park — great for families with children. Also home to the Katraj Lake and a butterfly garden.

FamilyWildlife

Vetal Hill Forest Reserve

4 km

A forested hillock with walking trails, city viewpoints, and a serene atmosphere — popular with early-morning trekkers and joggers.

TrekkingViews

Pashan Lake

3.5 km

A serene lake popular among birders, cyclists, and evening walkers. Migratory birds are common from November to February.

BirdsCyclingSunsets

Day Trips from Pune
🏔️

Lonavala & Khandala

Pune's favourite weekend escape — lush valleys, waterfalls, chikki shops, and ancient Karla Caves. Perfect in the monsoon and winter months.

65 km ~1.5 hrs drive Year-round
🏰

Sinhagad Fort

A historic hilltop fort 30km from Pune with panoramic views, a challenging trek, and famous local food stalls serving vada pav and curd.

30 km ~1 hr drive Oct–Mar best
🍓

Mahabaleshwar

Maharashtra's premier hill station, famous for strawberries, viewpoints like Arthur's Seat, and the sacred Krishnabai Temple.

120 km ~3 hrs drive Nov–May best
💧

Pawna Lake & Camping

A stunning reservoir surrounded by forts and hills — popular for lakeside camping, stargazing, and kayaking. Gorgeous at sunrise.

65 km ~1.5 hrs drive Year-round
🛕

Jejuri Temple

The golden temple of Lord Khandoba — a vibrant pilgrimage site where devotees throw turmeric powder. A unique and colourful cultural experience.

50 km ~1 hr drive Year-round
🌊

Bhimashankar Jyotirlinga

One of the 12 sacred Jyotirlingas set in a wildlife sanctuary — a spiritual trek through dense forests with rich biodiversity.

100 km ~3 hrs drive Year-round

  Getting Around

Auto-rickshaws and cabs (Ola, Uber) are widely available. Our front desk can arrange car rentals or a hotel cab for day trips.

  Best Time to Visit

October to February is ideal — cool and dry. Monsoon (July–September) brings lush greenery but check road conditions for hill trips.

  Local Food

Try Misal Pav, Sabudana Khichdi, and Puran Poli — iconic Maharashtrian dishes available at local restaurants within walking distance.

  Need Help?

Our 24-hour front desk can book cabs, recommend guides, and arrange day-trip itineraries. Just ask — we're happy to help!
